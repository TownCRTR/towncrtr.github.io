TownCRTR's Story

Writen by: gluester55 / Ethan [Owner]



Hi! I'm Ethan, the owner, and developer of TownCRTR. Founder, also, as that's a tad important to the story.


What inspired TownCRTR?

Sometime in 2019, "Dang.. ValkNet (valknet.minehut.gg) is so cool. Kinda inspiring.. Server host in a server host. Wait! A town creator! (I didn't know towny was a thing-) I went to the Minehut site, and bought a new server. I named it TownCreator, but it was too long. So, TownCRTR was born. CRTR meaning CReaToR. Now, I created it! Back then, I just wanted it *done*, and it showed. The spawn was ... ugly, made by me only, and rushed. The main script (skript) was buggy, and un-polished. Back then, I was also not as good with Skript, which made the process A) longer, and B) less ... good- certainly a thing, less-good. Anywho, it showed. My slacking showed, skills showed. Eventually, I redid the whole thing- hence V2. (current version as of making this is V2-3.1B, I believe.) Now, fast forward through many updates: we're here on Kingdoms.GG, though soon, we'll probably be moving ^v^


Thanks for reading, Ethan
